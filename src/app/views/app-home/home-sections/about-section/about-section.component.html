About Us - Miss Bronze and Gold Ireland

Miss Bronze and Gold Ireland is out to empower young women of all nationalities living in Ireland.

Miss Bronze and Gold Ireland First Edition took place 2014. The Second Edition will be held on Saturday 27th July at Ambassadors Hotel,Military Hill, Cork. The event will start at 6:00pm.

Miss Bronze and Gold Ireland was one of the highly anticipated Pageant launches in Ireland in 2014. It is one of the Largest multicultural pageant in Ireland

This is the second Edition and we plan to make it bigger and even more diverse this year. It is a beauty pageant with no weight, height or nationality restrictions.

It is very inclusive and everyone is welcome to participate regardless of your national. Our focus is not only beauty, we focus on beauty and brain.
